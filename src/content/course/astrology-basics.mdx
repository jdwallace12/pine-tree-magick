# Welcome to Astrology Basics!

This comprehensive course will take you from complete beginner to confident astrologer. You'll learn everything you need to know to understand and interpret your birth chart.

## What You'll Learn

In this course, we'll cover:

- **The fundamentals of astrology** and how it works
- **The 12 zodiac signs** and their characteristics  
- **Planetary influences** and their meanings
- **How to read your birth chart** step by step
- **Practical applications** for daily life

## Course Structure

The course is designed to be completed over 6 weeks, with one lesson per week. Each lesson includes:

- Video instruction
- Written materials
- Practice exercises
- Additional resources

## Getting Started

Begin with Lesson 1: Introduction to Astrology. Take your time with each concept and don't hesitate to review materials as needed.

Remember, astrology is a journey of self-discovery. Enjoy the process!
