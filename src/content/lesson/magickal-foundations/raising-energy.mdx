## Working with Energy

Once you are grounded, centered, and your intention is set, the next step is to raise energy to fuel your spell or ritual.

### Methods for Raising Energy:

- **Chanting/Singing:** Using the vibration of your voice.
- **Drumming/Music:** Using rhythm to build a crescendo.
- **Dancing/Movement:** Engaging the physical body.
- **Visualization:** Imagining a sphere of light growing brighter and more intense.

Once the energy peaks, you must direct it toward your intention ("releasing" the energy) by pushing it out into the universe, often visualizing it shooting out like an arrow or bursting like a firework.
