## What is a Sacred Space?

A sacred space is an area dedicated to your magickal practice, meditation, and spiritual centeredness. It doesn't have to be a whole room; it can be a corner, a small table (altar), or even a portable box.

### Key Elements of a Sacred Space:

1. **Cleanliness:** Keep it physically and energetically clean.
2. **Elemental Representation:** Earth (crystals/salt), Air (incense/feathers), Fire (candles), Water (a small bowl of water).
3. **Personal Connection:** Items that hold spiritual meaning to you.

Take some time today to find a spot in your home and claim it as your sacred space.
