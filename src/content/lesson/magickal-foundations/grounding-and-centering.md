## The Importance of Grounding

Before raising energy for magick, it's crucial to ground and center yourself. Grounding connects you to the Earth's stable energy, preventing you from becoming overwhelmed or "flighty." Centering brings your focus to your core.

### The Tree Root Visualization

1. Sit or stand comfortably with your feet flat on the floor.
2. Close your eyes and breathe deeply.
3. Visualize roots growing from the soles of your feet (or base of your spine if sitting), reaching deep into the Earth.
4. Feel the solid, nurturing energy of the Earth flowing up through these roots into your body.
5. Send any excess, nervous, or negative energy back down the roots to be neutralized by the Earth.
