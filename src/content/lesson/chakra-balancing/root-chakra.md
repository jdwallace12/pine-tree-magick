# The Root Chakra (Muladhara)

Located at the base of the spine, the Root Chakra is your foundation. It represents your sense of security, stability, and belonging.

## Key Characteristics

- **Color**: Red
- **Element**: Earth
- **Mantra**: LAM
- **Focus**: Survival and security

## Signs of Imbalance

When your Root Chakra is out of balance, you might feel anxious, fearful, or disconnected from the physical world.

## How to Balance

1.  **Earthing**: Spend time barefoot on the grass.
2.  **Red Foods**: Eat strawberries, tomatoes, or beets.
3.  **Affirmations**: "I am safe. I am grounded. I have everything I need."
