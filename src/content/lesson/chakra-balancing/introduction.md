# Welcome to your Chakra Balancing Journey!

In this introductory lesson, we will explore the history and significance of the chakra system. Understanding these energy centers is the first step toward greater self-awareness and healing.

## What are Chakras?

The word "Chakra" comes from Sanskrit and means "wheel" or "disk." They are focal points in the subtle body where energy (Prana) flows.

## What to Expect

In this course, we will:

1.  Learn about each of the 7 main chakras.
2.  Practice specific exercises to clear blockages.
3.  Implement daily rituals for energy maintenance.
